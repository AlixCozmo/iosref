Users with 3D Touch-compatible devices 3D Touch can access app quick actions and perform peek and pop actions. Only iPhones have 3D Touch. It is important to make all app functionality available without 3D Touch, so that users without 3D Touch devices can still access them.

On Mac and Apple Watch, this feature is known as Force Touch.
